Privacy Policy for Memmy Mobile Application


Effective Date: June 20th, 2023


Thank you for using Memmy! This Privacy Policy outlines how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Memmy mobile application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.


Information We Collect

Memmy does not collect or store any user data, except for the information necessary to provide push notifications to your device. This information may include your device's unique identifier (e.g., device token) and other technical information required for delivering notifications. We do not collect any personally identifiable information or track your activities within the App.


Use of Information

The information we collect for push notifications is solely used for the purpose of delivering notifications to your device. We do not use this information for any other purpose, such as advertising or tracking.


Servers and Data Control

Memmy connects to servers that we do not host or have control over. These servers are part of a federated system that enables interactions between servers. While we strive to ensure the security and privacy of your data within our App, we cannot guarantee the security or privacy practices of the external servers. Any data stored or processed on these servers is subject to the respective privacy policies and terms of service of those servers.


Adult Content

Memmy is a link aggregator platform, and while some instances within the federated system may host adult content, our App blocks such content by default. We take reasonable measures to prevent the display of explicit or adult content within the App. However, we cannot guarantee that all instances or links will adhere to these guidelines. It is the user's responsibility to exercise caution while accessing external content.


Third-Party Services

Memmy does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics tools that collect personal information or track your activities within the App. We prioritize user privacy and do not engage in any data sharing or tracking practices.


Children's Privacy

Memmy is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible. If you believe that we may have collected information from a child under the age of 13, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.


Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your information.


Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the privacy practices of Memmy, please contact us at support@memmy.app.


By using the Memmy mobile application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein.
